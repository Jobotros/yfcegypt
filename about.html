About YFC Egypt: A Brief History

YFC Egypt began in 2000, inspired by the support of Bob Moffett and John Sagherian. Initially established as a pioneering nation, it was part of the MENA region (Middle East and North Africa) before transitioning to the EMENA region (Europe, Middle East, and North Africa). In its early years, YFC Egypt operated under the Anglican Church until 2008, when it came under the Evangelical Fellowship of Egypt, continuing its mission of youth outreach and development.
